In Port Macquarie, ground improvement addresses the challenges posed by the region's alluvial and estuarine deposits, where loose sands and soft clays often fail to meet bearing capacity requirements under AS 5100 and AS 2870. Our approach integrates targeted techniques such as stone column design to reinforce compressible soils and vibrocompaction design to densify granular strata, mitigating settlement risks before structural loads are applied.

These methods prove essential for coastal residential subdivisions, commercial slabs on reclaimed land, and transport corridor earthworks where deep excavation is impractical. By combining vibrocompaction for near-surface sands with rigid inclusions or stone columns for deeper soft layers, we deliver engineered solutions that align with local council requirements for long-term foundation performance.
